About Us

Hi – I’m Steve Hudek, the Founder and Creative Director for LoSoBe Marketing & Design. I care about things like the environment, the education of our children, world affairs, animal welfare – the list goes on. What it really boils down to is that I care about the impact I, and my business, have on family and friends, community, and the world. I bring who I am to LoSoBe because I believe that one cannot separate the person from the business. Therefore, the business is a reflection of me – my values, ethics, and character.

In my work as a web developer and graphic designer, I came to know business owners who were passionate about what they were doing. They were doing good work to make the world a better place. But, they were struggling because they weren’t connecting with like-minded customers.

In order to bring about change, we need to connect with those who will support us in that change.

We at LoSoBe are passionate about helping you connect authentically with your customers. Our goal is to help you:

  • Look Good – Through design, advertising, leadership
  • Sound Good – Using communication, messaging, and connection
  • Be Good – Developing the relationship you have with your customers and the impact of your products and services.

Does the mere thought of promoting your business give you a funny feeling in the pit of your stomach? Perhaps even a bit of revulsion? Are you turned off by all the advertising and sales gimmicks out there? Don’t you wish there was a better way?

We feel your pain. Because we’ve been there and done that (we didn’t get a t-shirt, though!).

And because we’ve been there and done that, we’re here to guide, and help you, every step of the way.

We take the mystery and uncertainty out of presenting your business (and by extension, yourself) by creatively and consciously collaborating with you, and staying with you every step of the way.

You’re doing good in the world. Does anyone know it?

Give us a call and let us show you how you can Look Good, Sound Good and Be Good – and create better relationships.
